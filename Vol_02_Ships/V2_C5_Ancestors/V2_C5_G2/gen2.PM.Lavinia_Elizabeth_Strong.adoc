 Regent Worcester Chapter D.A.R.

notes: Grandma Sparrell (Lavinia Elizabeth Strong) was the daughter of Albert Randolph Strong, foreman of the Worcester roundhouse of the Worcester &amp; Nashua Railroad.

She was Regent of the Colonel Bigelow Chapter of the Daughters of the American Revolution in Worcester.

She was active in the Women's Association of the Universalist Church and was much involved in the acquisition of the Clara Barton birthplace farm in Oxford and the creation of Clara Barton State Park and Camp Clara Barton for diabetic girls.

She was also active in the Worcester Chapter of The Red Cross and the First Universalist Church.
