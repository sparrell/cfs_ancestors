notes: <p>John,aged 40, sailed 1635 from London in ship "Susan and Ellen" with wife and older children. He was listed as age 40 and his wife Martha 28, children John age 3 and Marie age 1.</p> <p>They settled first in Ipswich, where he was a proprietor. He was a</p> <p>draper, and also a husbandman. He later removed to Salem. He was listed</p> <p>as a commoner in 1641. He was one of the 27 richest inhabitants and had 2</p> <p>shares in Plum Island in 1664. His will was made 28 Aug and proved 28 Nov</p> <p>1672 at Salem. First two children born in England and the remainder in</p> <p>Salem, MA.</p> <p>&nbsp;</p>
